Facilities at Jerusalem College of Engineering

Jerusalem College of Engineering in Chennai offers state-of-the-art facilities to enhance the learning experience of students. Here are some of the key facilities available:

  • Well-equipped laboratories for various disciplines including engineering, science, and computer applications.
  • Modern classrooms with audio-visual aids to facilitate effective learning.
  • Extensive library with a vast collection of books, journals, and online resources.
  • Computer labs with high-speed internet connectivity for research and project work.
  • Workshops and seminar halls for conducting interactive sessions and workshops.
  • Sports facilities including playgrounds, indoor courts, and gymnasium for physical fitness and recreation.
  • Cafeteria serving nutritious and hygienic food for students and staff.
  • Transportation services for students and faculty members for easy commute to and from the college.
  • Medical facilities with qualified doctors and nurses to address any health concerns.
  • Wi-Fi connectivity across the campus for seamless access to online resources.
  • 24/7 security surveillance to ensure the safety and security of students and staff.

These facilities are designed to provide a conducive environment for learning, research, and overall development of the students at Jerusalem College of Engineering.
