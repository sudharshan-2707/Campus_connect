Jerusalem College - Placement Cell

The Placement Cell at Jerusalem College is dedicated to assisting students in securing employment opportunities that align with their skills, interests, and career goals. Our goal is to bridge the gap between academia and industry by facilitating interactions between students and potential employers.

We offer a wide range of services to our students, including:

  • Organizing campus recruitment drives by inviting reputed companies from various sectors.
  • Arranging pre-placement training sessions to enhance students' employability skills such as aptitude, communication, and interview techniques.
  • Providing career counseling and guidance to help students make informed decisions about their career paths.
  • Facilitating internships and industry projects to give students hands-on experience and exposure to real-world challenges.
  • Maintaining a strong alumni network to connect current students with successful alumni working in different industries.

Our Placement Cell works closely with both students and recruiters to ensure a seamless recruitment process. We have a proven track record of placing our students in top companies across various sectors including IT, engineering, finance, consulting, and more.

At Jerusalem College, we believe that a strong placement support system is essential for the holistic development and future success of our students.

Placement Process

The placement process at College Name is carefully designed to ensure that students are well-prepared for their careers. It involves several stages:

  1. Pre-placement Talks: Companies visit the campus to conduct pre-placement talks and provide students with information about job opportunities and company culture.
  2. Written Tests: Students participate in written tests to assess their aptitude, technical knowledge, and communication skills.
  3. Group Discussions: Qualified candidates engage in group discussions to evaluate their ability to communicate effectively, work in teams, and present their ideas.
  4. Technical and HR Interviews: Shortlisted candidates undergo technical and HR interviews with representatives from recruiting companies to assess their technical skills, problem-solving abilities, and suitability for the job role.
  5. Offer Letters: Successful candidates receive offer letters from companies, and the placement process concludes with students securing job placements.

Placement Training

College Name provides comprehensive placement training programs to enhance students' employability and prepare them for the recruitment process. The training includes:

  • Technical skills development
  • Mock interviews
  • Resume building workshops
  • Soft skills training
  • Personality development sessions

These training programs equip students with the necessary skills and confidence to excel in interviews and secure job offers from top companies.
